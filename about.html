Our Story

Built to make insurance clear and useful

A strong desire to offer good and reliable insuarance service in Kenya pushed Sammy Kochei to establish Royalsome insuarance Agency in 2019.it was first registered as Sammy Kochei Kipkurui Agency and later Royalsome insuarance agency the following year. Having worked in insuarance industry for over 10 years, Kochei had first hand experience of challenges faced by clients. While working in accounts, agency service,medical claims,general underwriting,adminstration,business adminstartion and marketing departments; the founder gained alot of experience in insuarance. This planted in him a need and strong urge to establish this reputable agency. The years in service has seen the firm increase its market share in Northrift region of Kenya. At the beggining of the year 2021, efforts have been made to widen the geographical regiona and influence to include South rift,Western,Nyanza and Nairobi.
